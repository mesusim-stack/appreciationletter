For my Granny,

I was thinking about us today about our conversations, the way you laugh at my stupid jokes, The way you get ragebaited by my playful teasing, and the way you always know how to make me feel better no matter what. I was reflecting on all these little things and especially how comfortable everything feels between us. It made me realize how much I value what we have.

The truth is, you've become really important to me. In all the small moments our morning texts, late night talks, even the comfortable silences I've found something real. And I'm not letting that go because of my own mistakes. You've become someone that I really cherish.

We talked, and I meant what I said. I'm still sorry for being a dummy. You know you're my favorite person to talk to, and I'm glad we're good.

Anyway, this is just a little note to hopefully give you a smile. Can't have my favorite granny feeling anything less than special.

Yours,

-Gramps
